About CasaNova

CasaNova Hookah Lounge is located at 2050 N Collins Blvd Ste 101, Richardson, TX 75080. Our state-of-the-art hookahs come in a variety of flavors, and we allow guests to BYOB (Bring Your Own Bottle).
